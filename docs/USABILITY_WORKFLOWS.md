# Usability Workflows

These workflows describe the paths the Hangar UI should keep smooth as features grow.

## Fleet Triage

- Start at the Hangar hub.
- Scan unit, operational, and flag counts.
- Inspect the Beast schematic and tap subsystem hotspots.
- Open a unit detail page from a unit card or schematic card.
- Return to hub or move into a bay without losing orientation.

## Mission Planning

- Open Missions from desktop sidebar or mobile bottom nav.
- Select a mission.
- Review objective, environment, constraint gauges, requisitioned units, and wishlist requisitions.
- Engage the mission lens.
- Return to Hangar and see the lensed objective plus spotlighted units.
- Clear the lens when the comparison is done.

## Acquisition Sourcing

- Open Quartermaster.
- Compare US distributor and import sourcing.
- Scan total tab, buy-next count, item statuses, source risk, and unit/capability links.
- Jump from a wishlist item to its unit or tech-tree unlock.

## Capability Planning

- Open Tech Tree.
- Hover, focus, or tap a capability node to spotlight dependencies and dependents.
- Check whether requirements are owned, received, or still sourced through Quartermaster.
- Jump to Quartermaster from a capability that needs parts.

## Field Knowledge Lookup

- Open Codex.
- Search by title, body, or tag.
- Filter by bay and confidence.
- Use tag chips to narrow the search.
- Follow linked unit and mission references back into operational views.

## Mobile Navigation

- Use the fixed bottom nav for all primary stations.
- Swipe the nav rail for bay shortcuts.
- Confirm each page avoids document-level horizontal scrolling at common phone widths.
